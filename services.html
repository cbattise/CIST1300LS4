Services


Each experience begins with a thorough consultation, followed by a tailored experience.
Our salon is departmentalized, meaning we have color specialists and style specialists. When booking online, if you wish to receive both color and cut, please book accordingly.

Our color services do not include a blow dry. An additional charge will be added for this service.
